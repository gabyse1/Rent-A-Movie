About us

Remember when renting a movie meant going to a store? Times have changed for the movie industry. With video rental chains a relic of the past, online shopping has become the new standard for renting movies to watch at home. Rent-A Movie is one such online movie rental service that allows you to browse through it's catalogue of movies, leave likes and comments or just make reservations for movies you like. It's cheap, easy and so much fun!
